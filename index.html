About Me

I am a dedicated, Portland-based actor and certified Stage Combat Choreographer, working consistently across stage and film. My acting experience covers a wide range of styles, from intense drama, to absurdest comedy, to live improv. I am a graduate of the Portland Actors Conservatory and specialized in combat training at the Acadamy of Theatrical Combat in Los Angeles. My commitment to the work is unwavering and I am known in the industry for bringing integrity to every project. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of training and studying with prominent industry figures, including Kristina Haddad, Ted Rooney, and Patrick Malone.

My work has been recognized by the acting community with three prestigious awards. I received the PAMTA (Portland Area Musical Theatre Award) for Outstanding Fight Choreographer for my work on Snow White at Northwest Children's Theater Company, demonstrating my technical mastery and storytelling ability in action. Additionally, I earned a Best Supporting Actor award from Twilight Theater Company for my portrayal of Robert in the comedic farce Boeing Boeing. Most recently I won a Labfest "Coaches' Choice" award for my short film, Darker Safer Ocean.

Whether I'm building a safe and dynamic fight sequence, writing a film script, or delivering a nuanced performance, I am fully invested in the art of collaboration and committed to delivering exceptional performances.
