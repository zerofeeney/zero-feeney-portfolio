About Zero

Zero Feeney is an accomplished performer, having trained at the **Portland Actor’s Conservatory** and **Lewis and Clark College**. A versatile artist, he is known for his compelling work on the stage, having collaborated with notable Portland-area companies including **defunkt**, **Vertigo**, **Milagro**, **Northwest Children’s Theater**, and **Twilight Theater Company**.

His work has garnered significant recognition, including a **Portland Area Musical Theatre Award (PAMTA)** for Northwest Children's Theater Company's 2016 production of *Snow White*. He also received the Twilight Theater Company's award for **Best Performance by a supporting male in 2016** for his portrayal of Robert in *Boeing Boeing*, and was previously recognized on the **2006/2007 Portland Drammy Awards' short list for Best Supporting Actor** for defunkt theatre's *The Trestle at Pope Lick Creek*. Zero is an alumnus of the **Academy of Theatrical Combat** in Los Angeles and continues to study martial arts and dance in his spare time.
