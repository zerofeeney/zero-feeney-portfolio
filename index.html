About Me

Zero Feeney is an experienced and versatile actor based in Portland, Oregon, specializing in complex character roles across film, television, and regional theatre. With a foundation in classical training, Zero brings intense dedication and transformative energy to every project.

Beyond acting, Zero is an **Award-Winning Stage Combat Choreographer**, certified in multiple disciplines of theatrical combat. This unique skillset ensures commitment to physical storytelling and safety on set. Zero is represented by the AME Talent Agency.
